2. Reverse Collar Tie
3. Overwrap Grip
4. Lat Knee
5. Twister Hook
6. Leg Drag Hook
7. False Grips
8. Reverse Kimura
9. DLR
10. Rev DLR
11. Mantis
12. Ankle lock
13. 2 on 1 Grips
14. Claw
15. 3/4 Nelson's
16. Figure of Four
